Flashless

A WebKit plug-in to display preview images for Flash based video services and block the rest.

Install/Uninstall

Requires Mac OS X 10.5 or 10.6, a WebKit browser (e.g. Safari).

  1. Download and open the disk image. Quit Safari.
  2. Copy the Plug-in to “/Library/Internet Plug-Ins” (for all users) or to “~/Library/Internet Plug-Ins” (for you only).
  3. Restart Safari. Enjoy.

To uninstall Flashless just quit Safari, move “Flashless.webplugin” from “/Library/Internet Plug-Ins” to the trash and restart Safari.

Supported Services

Basic usage (blocking, playing, removing) is available for all Flash content from every source.

Can I play videos in fullscreen mode?

Yes. Using Flashless in Safari 5 on Mac OS X Snow Leopard, you can play videos from all supported services in fullscreen mode.

Just click the double arrows on the lower right side of the video.

Why can’t I download videos from YouTube?

Sometimes videos from YouTube open in the browser window instead of downloading. If that happens, click the address bar, hold the option key and press the return key to start the download.

Known Issues

Although most sites work flawlessly with Flashless, some may have issues.

Facebook: Farmville, …

You may experience problems when trying to use some Facebook apps (e.g. Farmville).

To fix them follow these steps:

  1. In the Finder choose Go › Utilities.
  2. Start Terminal by double clicking its icon.
  3. Enter the following text or paste it. 
    defaults write de.uselessness.Flashless UCWhitelist -array-add "static.ak.fbcdn.net"
  4. Press Enter.
  5. Quit Terminal and restart Safari.

Facebook apps should work now.
