Download (.dmg, 210K)

Version 1.5, requires Mac OS X 10.5 or later, a Webkit browser (e.g. Safari).

  1. Download and open the disk image. Quit Safari.
  2. Copy the Plugin to “/Library/Internet Plug-Ins” (for all users) or to “~/Library/Internet Plug-Ins” (for you only).
  3. Restart Safari. Enjoy.

Features

Flashless blocks Flash content on a website. But it can display a preview for some popular video services. For some of them it can even download the video directly.

Usage

Flashless replaces all Flash elements on a site with an unobtrusive graphic. To show the original flash just click on the placeholder.

Blocked Flash ad.

For content from a supported video services (see Features) a preview image of the video is displayed. So you can see what video will be played before even loading the Flash element. The name of the service is displayed in the lower right corner.

Preview videos. (Video (cc) Harald Walker)

From some Sites you can directly download the video. Just hold the option key and click the arrow. Not all services support downloading at the moment.

Download videos directly.

Many more options available from the menu. You can remove elements from the site. You can play or remove all elements from the same source. And you can even let Flashless do it automatically for you.

Options from the menu.

To uninstall Flashless just quit Safari, move “Flashless.webplugin” from “/Library/Internet Plug-Ins” to the trash and restart Safari.
