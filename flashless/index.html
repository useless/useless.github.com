Flashless

A WebKit plug-in to display preview images for Flash based video services and block the rest.

Download (.dmg, 200K)

Version 1.5, requires Mac OS X 10.5 or later.

Open the disk image, copy the Plugin to “~/Library/Internet Plug-Ins/” and restart Safari.
